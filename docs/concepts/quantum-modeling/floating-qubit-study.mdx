#FloatingQubit Research

<Aside type="note" title="research notes">

This page documents the exploration and analysis of the Floating Qubit circuit model.

</Aside>

## background

The Floating Qubit is a transmon design that is not directly connected to ground and is capacitively coupled to the read resonator and XY control lines.

- `circuit_model_analysis/floating_1Q_coupled_XY.jl`
